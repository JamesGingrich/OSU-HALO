Bio

The Strokes are an American alternative rock band, formed in 1998 in New York. Their careers shot off with the release of their debut album 'Is This It' and were at the forefront of the garage rock revival of the early 21st century. The lineup of the band consists of Nick Valensi on lead guitar, Julian Casablancas on vocals, Fabrizio Moretti on drums, Albert Hammond Jr. on rhythm guitar and Nikolai Fraiture on bass guitar.
